# About Me

I am a Tenure-track Professor at the University of Seville (ES), an R&D Chemist, and a Deep-Tech Founder. I don't just view academia as a place for publishing papers; to me, **research is an incubator for real-world inventions**. 

My mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental laboratory discoveries and scalable industrial applications. I combine the rigor of academic science with the agility and vision of an entrepreneur, turning molecular innovations into market-ready technologies.

# Research Vision

I specialize in:

*   **Process Intensification & Continuous Flow Chemistry:** Moving from batch to continuous manufacturing to make chemical production safer, faster, and more sustainable.
*   **Green Chemistry & Sustainable Engineering:** Designing chemical processes that minimize waste, utilize renewable feedstocks, and drastically reduce carbon footprints.
*   **Hybrid organic-inorganic Nanomaterials:** Developing next-generation nanomaterials with precise biological or industrial targeting capabilities.
